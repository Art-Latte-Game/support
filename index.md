# Latte Art Game — Support

Welcome to the support page for **Latte Art Game**.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**How do I save my latte art?**
Tap the save button after creating your design. The app will ask for permission to save to your photo library.

**How do I reset a design?**
Start a new pour from the challenge or free pour menu to begin with a fresh cup.

**The app is not loading ads correctly.**
Make sure you have an active internet connection. Ads require network access to load.

**I found a bug or have a suggestion.**
Please open an issue on our [GitHub page](https://github.com/Art-Latte-Game/support/issues) or contact us at the email below.

## Privacy Policy

Latte Art Game is committed to protecting your privacy.

### We Do Not Collect Personal Data

Latte Art Game does not require an account, login, or any personal information to use. We do not collect, store, or share your name, email address, or any other personally identifiable information.

### Advertising

The app uses Google AdMob to display ads. AdMob may collect certain device information and advertising identifiers to serve relevant ads. For more details, see [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

You may opt out of personalized advertising through your device settings:
- **iOS**: Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking

### Photos

The app allows you to save latte art creations to your photo library. This requires your permission and is done entirely on your device. We do not access, upload, or store your photos.

### Data Storage

All game data (progress, scores, creations) is stored locally on your device. Nothing is transmitted to external servers.

### Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13.

### Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
